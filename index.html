Features

Apple ID Signing

Sign apps using your personal or developer Apple ID directly on your device.

Certificate Signing

Import and use .p12 and .mobileprovision files for enterprise-grade signing.

JIT Enabler

Enable Just-In-Time compilation for emulators and VMs without needing a computer.

Community Sources

Full support for AltStore repositories. Add and manage external sources with ease.

App Browser

Discover and explore thousands of apps from your favorite community sources.

Metadata Reader

Inspect bundle IDs, version history, and detailed app info before installing.

Theming Engine

Personalize your experience with OLED Dark, Light, Indigo, and Forest themes.

News Feed

Stay updated with news and release notes directly from app developers.

Entitlement Support

Supports the Increased Memory Limit entitlement.
